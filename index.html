The solar system consists of the Sun, eight planets, their moons, and a variety of smaller objects like asteroids and comets, all bound together by the Sun's gravitational pull. Each planet orbits the Sun at different distances, creating a diverse range of environments, from the scorching heat of Mercury to the icy realms of the outer planets.
